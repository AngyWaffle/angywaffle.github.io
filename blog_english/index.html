Why is having your phone in school a good thing?

fomo

Not having your phone increases fomo, fear of missing out, because you can miss out on plans that your friends make. Knowledge of this increases stress and makes you unable to focus the same. This leads to decreased workflow.

Entertaining education

On phones you can download apps that can make the learning experience both fun and educational for the students. Apps like Duolingo which is used to learn new languages for students and with all the built in features of the app, it makes it more fun for the students.

Availabilty

The most important thing about your phone is calling and texting, that is what it was made for at first and being available at all times is important because emergencies happen all the time. Not having your phone during class means that an emergency could occur, and you won't be available. Maybe your mother is in the hospital and needs your help or there is a fire nearby and you need or get away asap.

Teaching tool

Having a mobile phone in class kan be usefull as a tool. You can get information just like a pc and you can use it while the internet is offline, and mobile phones are more abeling to the younger population at this time and age.
